    Boundary¶

    This chain index is a traceability surface only.

    It does not grant:

    • proof authority by itself
    • truth authority by itself
    • clinical authority
    • execution authority
    • governance-bypass authority
    • external-auditor acceptance
    • journal acceptance
    • community acceptance
