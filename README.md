### Bounded autonomous-agent spine

The v0.20 to v0.26 contracts are composed by v0.27 into a restart-safe sequence of separately bounded cycles.

```text
persistent mission
  -> observation and belief
  -> semantic plan and independent verification
  -> bounded memory and learning
```

Every cycle requires finite cost, finite steps, finite duration, a finite lease, fresh cycle authorization and an exact host-license binding.

Pause, terminate and handover remain foreground controls.


## What KuuOS does not claim

KuuOS is not currently:

- a production AGI platform or an unbounded background agent;
- arbitrary shell, network, repository or deployment authority;
- a universal global-context graph, shortest-path policy engine or single-winner truth system;
- a standalone medical diagnostic, triage, treatment or medical-act authorization system;
- proof that Qi is a physical substance, nor a denial of East Asian medical reasoning;
- an externally accepted proof merely because Lean or CI succeeds;
- a physical quantum Markov semigroup, exact physical vacuum construction or exact WORLD simulator;
- a system in which relative entropy becomes ontological distance or a truth score;
- a system in which Petz recovery overwrites WORLD, MemoryOS or causal history;
- a system in which excitation vectors become action instructions;
- a system in which vacuum-expectation candidates become empirical facts, beliefs, plans, action authority or WORLD updates by themselves;
- a system in which memory, validation, curvature, low free energy, verification, receipts or runtime success become truth by themselves.

The medical boundary is modality-neutral.

Repository validation is separate from professional diagnosis, treatment decisions and institutional responsibility.

## Development rule

```text
additive lineage
same-root binding for protected surfaces
append-only evidence
explicit versioning
fail closed on stale or substituted state
finite authority and finite resource scope
independent observation after effect
visible uncertainty, dissent, residue and cost
no silent promotion from candidate to authority
```

New layers should state:

1. what they consume;
2. what they produce;
3. who owns the phase;
4. what authority is required;
5. what persists;
6. how replay and stale state are handled;
7. what the validator proves;
8. what remains external or unproved;
9. what the layer explicitly cannot authorize.
