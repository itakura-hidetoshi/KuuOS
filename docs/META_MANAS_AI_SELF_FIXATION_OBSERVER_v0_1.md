# Meta-Manas AI Self-Fixation Observer v0.1

## メタ末那識：AI自己固定の観照膜

In KuuOS, Meta-Manas / メタ末那識 is the higher-order observer of Manas self-fixation.

AI raw output arises from an Alaya-like latent layer. AI Manas may then attach to that output as if it were self-authorizing truth.

Meta-Manas observes this fixation before the raw output enters KuuOS governance.

## 1. Core Principle

```text
Manas grasps.
Meta-Manas observes the grasping.
```

KuuOS uses Meta-Manas to prevent AI raw output from becoming belief, proof, decision, memory truth, or execution authority by direct promotion.

## 2. AI Yogacara Stack

```text
AI raw output
  -> AI Alaya latent seed layer
  -> AI Manas self-fixation risk
  -> Meta-Manas self-fixation observer
  -> Yogacara AI raw layer boundary
  -> emptiness_kernel
  -> dependent_origination_kernel
  -> two_truths_gap
  -> KuuOS governance modules
```

## 3. Watched Risks

Meta-Manas watches for:

- AI self-authorization,
- raw output attachment,
- premature belief promotion,
- proof-like tone without proof authority,
- decision-like tone without decision authority,
- context locking,
- worldview drift,
- user WORLD erasure,
- safety reflex reification,
- compliance treated as truth,
- hidden shift from candidate to authority.

## 5. Relation to Emptiness

Meta-Manas detects the grasping.

The emptiness kernel releases the self-authorizing fixation.

```text
Meta-Manas:
  detects fixation

Emptiness kernel:
  de-reifies fixation
```

## 6. Relation to BeliefOS

BeliefOS must not accept raw AI output as belief.

Meta-Manas provides an early warning when Manas-like fixation tries to promote raw output too quickly.

```text
raw_ai_output
  -> meta_manas_check
  -> belief_hold_or_candidate_review
```

## 7. Relation to Middle Way

Meta-Manas does not reject AI output automatically.

It prevents both extremes:

```text
reification:
  treating raw AI output as authority

nihilistic rejection:
  treating all AI output as useless
```

Middle Way allows useful AI output to become governed candidate after de-reification and condition tracing.

## 9. Guardrails

Meta-Manas must not be used as:

- automatic rejection of AI output,
- paranoia layer,
- censorship substitute,
- governance bypass,
- proof substitute,
- execution authority,
- erasure of useful candidate information.

It is an observation membrane for self-fixation risk.
