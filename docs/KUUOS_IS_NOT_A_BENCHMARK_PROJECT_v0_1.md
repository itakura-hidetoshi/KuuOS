# KuuOS Is Not a Benchmark Project v0.1

## Core Clarification

KuuOS is not primarily a benchmark project.

Benchmarks in KuuOS are auxiliary observation instruments.

They may help inspect governance behavior, but they do not define the system's essence.

## What KuuOS Is

KuuOS is a relational operating architecture for AI governance.

Its core is:

- emptiness as non-reification
- dependent origination as traceable relationality
- two truths gap as non-collapse boundary
- middle way as stabilization
- Mandala as multi-world inclusion
- governance before authority
- provenance before assertion
- observation before escalation

## What Benchmarks Are

Benchmarks are:

- observation tools
- stress-test instruments
- governance probes
- diagnostic surfaces

Benchmarks are not:

- the core OS
- the source of authority
- the replacement for architecture
- the source of truth
- the final arbiter of governance quality

## Correct Placement

```text
KuuOS constitution
    ↓
Governance architecture
    ↓
Runtime boundary surfaces
    ↓
Observation / audit / benchmark probes
```

Benchmark probes sit downstream of the architecture.

They do not define the architecture.

## Boundary

A benchmark result may inform review.

It must not silently become:

- authority
- theorem closure
- deployment permission
- institutional approval
- OS identity

## Reviewer Guidance

Review KuuOS first as an operating architecture.

Review benchmark artifacts second as auxiliary diagnostic tools.
