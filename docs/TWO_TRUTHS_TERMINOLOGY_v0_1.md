# Two Truths Terminology v0.1

KuuOS uses the Buddhist two-truths terminology as follows:

- 勝義諦 / paramartha-satya
- 世俗諦 / samvrti-satya

In KuuOS, 勝義諦 should not be translated as a supreme substance or a fixed ultimate entity. It names the non-reifying truth of emptiness.

世俗諦 names the conventional operating surface: language, models, rules, proofs, plans, metrics, artifacts, and world-model descriptions.

中道 preserves the living distinction between 勝義諦 and 世俗諦 without collapsing either side.

Therefore, future KuuOS public-core documents should prefer:

```text
勝義諦 / paramartha-satya
世俗諦 / samvrti-satya
```

over vague wording such as:

```text
ultimate truth
conventional truth
```

when the distinction matters.
