# Physical Quantum Qi Runtime Baseline Statement v0.1

This statement summarizes the public KuuOS baseline for the Physical Quantum Qi runtime layer.

## Baseline declaration

Physical Quantum Qi is a non-sovereign, evidence-bound relation-flow layer on the conventional appearance side of KuuOS.

It is not emptiness itself, not a mystical substance, not a creator principle, and not an execution authority.

```text

  -> Sigma_ws
```

## PhysicalQi boundary

`PhysicalQi` requires the evidence bundle for:

- non-reification of `K`,
- `delta_rel` in `K_perp`,
- string/brane organization,
- gauge connection,
- curvature and holonomy residue,
- current coupling,
- Ward or leak identity,
- density state, Hamiltonian, Lindblad/open-system generator,
- entropy production and non-equilibrium free energy,
- DPI gap and recovery margin,
- MGAP4D `33/20` mass-gap stability floor.

`FullPathQi` additionally requires SK/FV history evidence: plus/minus branches, influence functional, memory kernel, noise kernel, backaction, noncommutative operation history, and path-measure normalization.

## Mass-gap role

The MGAP4D internal-normalized `33/20` gap is a stability floor for the `K_perp` world-phase domain on which Qi flow is evaluated.

It does not generate Qi, does not reify `K`, and does not replace Ward/leak, recoverability, or SK/FV history checks.

## Authority boundary

A validated Qi packet grants no:

- proof authority,
- ontology authority,
- clinical authority,
- execution authority,
- belief commit authority,
- memory overwrite authority,
- world-root rewrite authority,
- safety override authority.

Qi may inform downstream KuuOS systems as a flow signal: leak, residue, backaction, recoverability, irreversibility, entropy cost, DPI loss, holonomy distortion, and downgrade reason.
