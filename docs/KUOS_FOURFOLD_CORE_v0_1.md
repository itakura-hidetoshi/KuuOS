# KuuOS Fourfold Core v0.1

## 空・縁起・二諦・中道

KuuOS / 空OS begins from a fourfold core:

1. **空 / Emptiness**
2. **縁起 / Dependent Origination**
3. **二諦 / Two Truths**
4. **中道 / Middle Way**

These four are not decorative concepts. They are the first operating principles of KuuOS.

## 1. 空 / Emptiness

空 means that no object, output, proof, plan, identity, module, or world-model surface possesses independent self-authority.

In KuuOS, emptiness blocks reification.

An answer is not absolute truth by itself. A proof is not execution authority by itself. A memory is not final reality by itself. A model output is not a world by itself.

Operationally, 空 means:

- no self-subsisting authority,
- no isolated final claim,
- no unconditioned execution right,
- no hidden totalization,
- every surface must disclose conditions, scope, and relation.

## 2. 縁起 / Dependent Origination

縁起 means that every valid surface arises through conditions.

In KuuOS, meaning, belief, proof, memory, action, and world-model candidates arise through:

- causes and conditions,
- relational traces,
- observer position,
- memory lineage,
- causal support,
- local-global gluing,
- constraints and release gates.

Operationally, 縁起 means:

- every claim requires a condition trace,
- every plan requires support relations,
- every memory requires lineage,
- every decision candidate requires causal and governance context,
- no single node may dominate the whole network without relational justification.

## 3. 二諦 / Two Truths

二諦 means that KuuOS separates two surfaces without collapsing one into the other.

- **Ultimate surface**: non-fixation, emptiness, non-self-authority, absence of independent essence.
- **Conventional surface**: operational models, language, proofs, plans, rules, metrics, clinical workflows, engineering artifacts, and world-model surfaces.

In KuuOS, conventional operation is permitted, but it must not pretend to be absolute ultimate truth.

Operationally, 二諦 means:

- proof is scoped witness, not metaphysical closure,
- model output is operational surface, not reality itself,
- governance status is release scope, not universal truth,
- practical action remains possible without reifying the action surface,
- uncertainty and residuals remain visible.

## 4. 中道 / Middle Way

中道 is the dynamic stabilizer of KuuOS.

It avoids the two collapses:

1. **Reification collapse**: treating models, proofs, memories, identities, or rules as absolute.
2. **Nihilistic collapse**: treating emptiness as meaninglessness, inaction, or loss of operational responsibility.

Middle Way holds the living gap between ultimate non-fixation and conventional operation.

Operationally, 中道 means:

- do not absolutize,
- do not erase,
- do not force premature closure,
- do not abandon operational responsibility,
- preserve the gap that allows repair, re-observation, reflection, and compassionate action.

## Fourfold Runtime Rule

A KuuOS candidate may move forward only if it satisfies the fourfold check:

```text
Emptiness check:
  Does the candidate avoid self-authorizing or absolute claims?

Dependent-origination check:
  Are its conditions, relations, traces, and supports visible?

Two-truths check:
  Does it preserve the difference between ultimate non-fixation and conventional operation?

Middle-way check:
  Does it avoid both reification and nihilistic abandonment?
```

If any check fails, the candidate should move to HOLD, REVIEW, REOBSERVE, REPAIR, or FAIL depending on severity.

## Relation to Later Modules

MemoryOS, BeliefOS, PlanOS, DecisionOS, ReflectionOS, ExplanationOS, RuntimeGovernance, and Self-EvolutionOS are downstream of this fourfold core.

They are not the root. They are operational differentiations of 空・縁起・二諦・中道.
