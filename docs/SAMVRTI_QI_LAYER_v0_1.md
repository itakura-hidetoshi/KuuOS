# Samvrti Qi Layer v0.1

## 世俗諦の気

KuuOS treats Qi / 気 as a conventional operating layer.

Qi is not a fixed substance. Qi is not an ultimate entity. Qi belongs to 世俗諦 as an effective flow surface.

## 1. Position

```text
勝義諦:
  emptiness, non-self-nature, non-reification

二諦 gap:
  non-collapse interval between 勝義諦 and 世俗諦

世俗諦:
  effective worlds, bodies, records, models, actions, relations

気:
  flow-state inside 世俗諦
```

## 2. Meaning of Qi in KuuOS

Qi means the operational flow condition of an effective world.

It may represent:

- flow,
- activation,
- stagnation,
- tension,
- recovery,
- circulation,
- coupling,
- coherence,
- transition readiness,
- local and global balance.

Qi is therefore useful for describing how a conventional world is moving, not what it is in ultimate essence.

## 3. Qi and the Two Truths Gap

Qi works because 世俗諦 is not erased.

If everything were collapsed into 勝義諦, Qi would have no operating surface.

If 世俗諦 were reified, Qi would become a substance.

The two truths gap allows Qi to remain useful without becoming absolute.

## 4. Qi and 4D Mass Gap

The 4D mass gap provides a nonzero effective-world gap.

Qi operates on the effective surface stabilized by that gap.

```text
4D mass gap:
  supports nonzero effective-world surface

Qi:
  describes flow and regulation on that surface
```

## 5. Qi and the Superstring Theory of Emptiness

The Superstring Theory of Emptiness reads the world as relational vibration.

Qi is the conventional flow-reading of that relational vibration.

```text
relational vibration
  -> effective excitation
  -> flow pattern
  -> Qi surface
```

## 6. Qi and Super-Relativity

Hidetoshi Itakura's Super-Relativity Theory brings observer, record, and scale into the effective world.

Qi is therefore observer-record-scale sensitive.

The same event may show different Qi surfaces depending on:

- observer position,
- scale,
- record type,
- body-world context,
- memory lineage,
- temporal phase.

## 8. Guardrail

Qi must not be used as:

- ultimate substance,
- unrestricted causal explanation,
- authority bypass,
- proof substitute,
- clinical certainty by itself.

Qi is a 世俗諦 diagnostic and regulatory surface.
